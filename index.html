I am a postdoctoral research associate at the School of Computer Science and Engineering, University of New South Wales. My research interest includes formal language, automata theory, evolutionary computation and their applications in program analysis and software security. I am now working with the open-source pointer analysis and program depedence analysis tool SVF and the open-source CFL-reachability analysis tool POCR.
